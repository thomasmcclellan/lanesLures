Frequently Asked Questions:


How do I buy a fly?

Hover your mouse over the lure that you like, let the car flip over (if you are on a mobile device, click the card to flip it over), select the quanity and click on the 'Add to Cart' button. The lure and quanity will automatically be added to your cart.

When you are ready to purchase, click on the cart icon and select either user or guest checkout. Your cart will show on the right. Fill out the form and push the 'Submit' button.


Do I need to sign in to use this site?

No! You do not need to sign in to use this site! However, if you are a repeat customer, it is more convenient for you to have an account. If you prefer, you are welcome to use this entire site without having to signin or signup.


What do I need to do to get my picture on this site?

If you use one of our lures to catch a fish, take a picture of your catch and email it to us. If we select it, we will put it on the website, AND, we will give you a free lure!
